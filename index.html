À cette époque, à la fin du XVIIIe siècle, la France est une monarchie absolue de droit divin.
Many faiths believed that some day a messiah would appear and end all wars, famines and even death itself.

The most important military invention in the history of China was gunpowder.

But the notion that humankind could do so by discovering new knowledge and inventing new tools was worse than ludicrous—it was hubris.

Science, industry and military technology intertwined only with the advent of the capitalist system and the Industrial Revolution. Once this relationship was established, however, it quickly transformed the world.

The story of the Tower of Babel, the story of Icarus, the story of the Golem and countless other myths taught people that any attempt to go beyond human limitations would inevitably lead to disappointment and disaster.
